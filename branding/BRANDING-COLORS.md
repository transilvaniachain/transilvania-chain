# Branding Colors — Transilvania Chain

This document defines the official color palette for the Transilvania Chain brand.  
All digital and print materials must follow these specifications to ensure visual consistency.

---

## 1. Primary Colors

### **Royal Gold**
- HEX: `#D4AF37`
- RGB: `212, 175, 55`
- Usage: Logo accents, premium highlights, key visual elements.

### **Deep Black**
- HEX: `#0A0A0A`
- RGB: `10, 10, 10`
- Usage: Backgrounds, typography, contrast elements.

### **Metallic Gray**
- HEX: `#2E2E2E`
- RGB: `46, 46, 46`
- Usage: Secondary backgrounds, UI elements, shadows.

---

## 2. Secondary Colors

### **Soft Gold**
- HEX: `#E8D18B`
- RGB: `232, 209, 139`
- Usage: Subtle highlights, borders, secondary accents.

### **Charcoal Gray**
- HEX: `#1A1A1A`
- RGB: `26, 26, 26`
- Usage: Panels, cards, UI blocks.

### **Warm White**
- HEX: `#F5F5F2`
- RGB: `245, 245, 242`
- Usage: Light backgrounds, print materials.

---

## 3. Color Usage Rules

- Gold must never be replaced with yellow or orange tones.
- Black must remain pure and deep; avoid washed-out grays.
- Gradients are not allowed unless explicitly approved.
- Backgrounds must maintain high contrast with the logo.

---

## 4. Accessibility

- Minimum contrast ratio for text: **4.5:1**
- Gold should not be used for small text.
- Black or white should be used for primary typography.

---

## 5. Legal Notice

All brand colors are part of the protected visual identity of Transilvania Chain.  
Unauthorized use is prohibited.

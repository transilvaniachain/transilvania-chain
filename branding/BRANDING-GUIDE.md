This document defines the official visual identity standards for the Transilvania Chain brand, including logo usage, color palette, proportions, and restrictions.
It ensures consistency, recognizability, and legal protection across all media and platforms.

1. Official Logo
The primary Transilvania Chain logo is included in this directory under the filename:

logo.png (standard version)

The logo is a protected visual asset and may not be altered, redistributed, or used without permission.

2. Clear Space Requirements
To preserve clarity and visual impact, the logo must always be surrounded by a clear space equal to the height of the letter T in the monogram.

No text, icons, or graphic elements may intrude into this protected area.

3. Minimum Size
To maintain legibility:

Digital
Minimum height: 120px

Print
Minimum height: 25mm

Below these thresholds, ornamental details may become unclear.

5. Approved Logo Variations
✔ Allowed
Full-color logo on black background

✖ Not Allowed
Changing the logo colors

Adding effects (shadows, gradients, glows)

Distorting proportions

Rotating or flipping the logo

Using the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds

7. Prohibited Usage
The logo may not be used for:

Commercial products without written permission

Unauthorized tokenization or NFTs

Branding for unrelated entities

Political, religious, or controversial contexts

Any misleading or harmful association

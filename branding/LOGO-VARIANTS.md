# Logo Variants — Transilvania Chain

This document defines all approved versions of the Transilvania Chain logo and the rules governing their usage.  
Only the variants listed here are permitted for official, public, or commercial use.

---

## 1. Primary Logo (Full Color)

**Filename:** `logo.png`  
**Description:**  
The main version of the Transilvania Chain logo, featuring the gold monogram and ornamental crest on a dark background.

**Usage:**  
- Official documents  
- Website headers  
- Presentations  
- Branding materials  
- Digital and print media  

**Backgrounds allowed:**  
- Black  
- White  
- Deep gray (`#0A0A0A` or `#1A1A1A`)

---

## 2. Monochrome Gold Version

**Filename:** `logo-gold.png` (optional future asset)  
**Description:**  
A simplified version of the logo rendered entirely in Royal Gold (`#D4AF37`).

**Usage:**  
- Minimalist layouts  
- Embossed or engraved effects  
- Premium print materials  
- Watermarks  

**Backgrounds allowed:**  
- Black  
- Deep gray  

---

## 3. Monochrome White Version

**Filename:** `logo-white.png` (optional future asset)  
**Description:**  
A white-only version of the logo for high-contrast applications.

**Usage:**  
- Dark backgrounds  
- UI elements  
- Overlays on images  

**Backgrounds allowed:**  
- Black  
- Dark gray  
- Gold (only if contrast is sufficient)

---

## 4. Inverted Version (Optional)

**Filename:** `logo-inverted.png`  
**Description:**  
A version where the background is light and the monogram is dark, used only when the primary logo cannot be applied.

**Usage:**  
- Light-themed documents  
- Print materials with white backgrounds  

**Restrictions:**  
- Must not replace the primary logo in official branding  
- Should be used sparingly  

---

## 5. Minimal Crest Version (Optional Future Asset)

**Filename:** `logo-minimal.png`  
**Description:**  
A simplified crest without ornamental details, used for small-scale applications.

**Usage:**  
- Favicons  
- App icons  
- Social media avatars  
- Small UI elements  

**Minimum size:**  
- 32×32 px (digital)  
- 8 mm (print)

---

## 6. Forbidden Variants

The following modifications are **strictly prohibited**:

### ✖ Color changes  
No recoloring to yellow, orange, blue, or any non-approved palette.

### ✖ Distortion  
No stretching, compressing, skewing, or rotating.

### ✖ Effects  
No shadows, glows, gradients, neon effects, or filters.

### ✖ Background interference  
No placement on busy, low-contrast, or patterned backgrounds.

### ✖ Partial logo extraction  
No using only letters, only ornaments, or cropped fragments.

---

## 7. File Naming Standards

To maintain consistency, all logo files must follow this naming convention:
logo.png
logo-gold.png
logo-white.png
logo-inverted.png
logo-minimal.png
